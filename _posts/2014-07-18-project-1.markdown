description: PP Filler Masterbatch is a poly propylene based masterbatch containing 75%~80% calcium carbonate. It is natural white with excellent dispersity. It can be mixed with Poly propylene resin for extrusion, thermal forming, injection etc. and is easy to disperse with good compatibility. It functions as a modifier to improve the heat resistance, flexural strength, size stability and printability. It also reduces heat release from burning and there force prevents further environmental pollution.
